Fettuccine Alfredo

A classic creamy pasta dish with a rich, buttery sauce.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook the fettuccine according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan over medium heat, melt the butter and add garlic. Cook for about 1 minute.
  3. Pour in the heavy cream and bring to a gentle simmer.
  4. Gradually stir in the Parmesan cheese until melted and smooth.
  5. Add the cooked fettuccine to the sauce, tossing to coat evenly.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with fresh parsley if desired.
